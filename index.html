ABOUT US

EVO1 CONSTRUCTION SERVICES was established on September 25, 2014. Over the years, the company specializes in contracting Renovation Projects and Industrial Cleaning Services. Founded by Engr. Jay Esguerra, who has 15 years of experience in Aluminum Composite Panel, waterproofing and paiting works, HVAC systems and cleanroom Construction and Trading products such as roofing, doors, windows, ceiling and various types of sun louvers and aluminum products, the company grew together with group of installers who has more than several years of experience of installation works. The knowledge gained especially in the strip installation works paved the way for the incorporation of the private limited company.


We are the top dealer of DML Products in the Philippines with an annual project of 10-20 Million.


Multiline ALUCOBEST AND WORLDBOND & ALUMART top distributor of Aluminum Composite Materials with annual sales of PHP 5-10 Million. With complete support of our manufacturer giving 10 years to 25 years warranty.


Mission

To provide the highest quality services and after sales support to all our clients while promoting the company’s products and services. EVO1 CONSTRUCTION SERVICES aims to help contribute to the public by charity works and be a good corporate citizen.
